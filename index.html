01 / Mission

Our charter

Bootstrapped · Bengaluru
Why we exist

Modern conflict moves in minutes. India's frontline units deserve indigenous aerial combat systems — not legacy imports retrofitted for a battlespace they were never designed for.

Taiyo Aerospace was founded in 2024 to develop sovereign aerospace capabilities for India. Our first product, Vritra, is a jet-powered, AI-piloted, man-portable platform built from the ground up for tactical response and time-sensitive strike. We design our airframes, write our flight code, and intend to build our own engines.

02 / Problem

The sensor-to-shooter gap

Time-sensitive targets

Mobile threats — rocket artillery, air defence, command vehicles — shoot and scoot in minutes. Today, infantry units find them with slow drones, then call for separate strike support. The result is a 30–45 minute lag that lets high-value targets escape.

Tactical Drones
< 150 km/h

Too slow and easily detected by radar. Can observe but cannot engage — forced to wait for fire support.

Strategic UAVs
SCARCE

MALE/HALE platforms are high-value assets. Cannot be risked at low altitude and rarely available on demand.

Manned Aircraft
30–45 MIN

Too expensive for hunting small, fleeting targets. In denied airspace or remote terrain, arrival can take over thirty minutes.

05 / System

System architecture

Stealth · Modular · Open
SUBSYSTEM / 01

Airframe & Stealth

  • Carbon-fibre monocoque unibody
  • Continuous curvature + edge alignment scatters radar
  • Serpentine S-duct inlet hides the turbine face
  • Detachable wings via quick-release pins
  • Simulated RCS < 0.01 m²
SUBSYSTEM / 02

Propulsion

  • JetCat Pro P-250 micro-turbojet
  • ~25 kg thrust, multi-fuel (Jet-A1 / diesel)
  • Eases logistics for forward-deployed units
  • Hot-pit turnaround under 10 minutes
  • Roadmap: indigenous Indian-made turbine
SUBSYSTEM / 03

Overmier AI & Avionics

  • Pixhawk Cube Orange+ flight controller
  • NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano AI compute
  • Real-time ATR for military vehicle ID
  • EMI-hardened decision chamber
  • GPS-denied nav via visual + dead reckoning
06 / Status

Where we are

Ground testing
70% Scale Prototype · Validated

Aerodynamics validated. Carbon-fibre monocoque in manufacturing.

We designed, built, and flew a 70% scale prototype to validate our aerodynamic design. Yaw response, climb performance, pitch authority, and surface drag all met or exceeded targets.

The full-scale carbon-fibre monocoque airframe is now in manufacturing. JetCat P-250 procurement is underway. Overmier AI integration is planned for the production airframe upon completion.

07 / Roadmap

Propulsion roadmap

Incremental de-risking
PHASE / 01 CURRENT

Electric Ducted Fan

Prototype validation of airframe, avionics, and AI systems at reduced speed. Low-risk flight envelope exploration.

PHASE / 02 NEXT

COTS micro-turbojet integration. ~25 kg thrust. Full 450 km/h dash capability. Multi-fuel (Jet-A / diesel).

Indigenous Turbine

Indian-developed and manufactured engine for strategic independence, optimised thrust, and supply-chain sovereignty.
